# Analysis Folder

### `/sales`
- Sales analysis, revenue trends, forecasting, and payment method breakdowns.
- Cohort analysis and AOV (average order value) evaluation.

### `/product`
- Product category analysis and trends.
- Customer review scores by product category.
- Freight cost vs. weight/dimensions analysis.
- Delivery delays vs. weight/dimensions.
  
### `/delivery`
- Delivery performance analysis.
- Delivery time classification and review correlation.
- State-level logistics insights and high-delay regions.
  
### `/customer`
- Analysis related to customer insights and segmentation.
- Includes sentiment analysis, topic extraction, clustering, and revenue-at-risk modelling.
