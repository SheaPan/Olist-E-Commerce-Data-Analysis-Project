## 5. Strategic Insights – Connecting the Dots

- **Revenue Concentration:** São Paulo dominates; small issues scale fast  
- **Products Drive Growth:** Watches & Gifts, Health & Beauty, Electronics  
- **Customer Risk:** 22% at risk, high-value accounts critical  
- **Delivery Drives Satisfaction:** Operational delays directly affect reviews

**Executive Story:** Growth is strong, but **revenue and satisfaction are vulnerable** without targeted actions.

---

## 6. Recommendations – Executive Action Plan

1. **Geographic Expansion:** Recruit sellers in Northern/Northeastern states  
2. **Peak Season Optimization:** Align promotions and inventory with spikes  
3. **Customer Retention:** Target high-risk and high-value accounts proactively  
4. **Delivery Optimization:** Prioritise low-value shipments, investigate bottlenecks  
5. **Product & Payment Strategy:** Promote top categories, bundle products, diversify payments  
6. **Continuous Monitoring:** Track satisfaction trends, delivery performance, and revenue risks

## Conclusion – Data-Driven Storytelling

Olist’s story is **one of growth with caution**:  

- Strong revenue but **concentrated risk**  
- Customer dissatisfaction exists in pockets  
- Delivery inefficiencies impact satisfaction  
- Opportunities exist in **product and payment strategies**  

**Actionable Story:** By connecting **sales, product, customer, and delivery insights**, Olist can **protect revenue, improve satisfaction, and scale operations efficiently**.
